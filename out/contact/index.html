Frequently Asked Questions

Is root canal treatment painful?

With modern anaesthesia and rotary techniques, RCT is no more uncomfortable than a regular filling. Most patients are surprised by how painless it is.

How long does a dental implant last?

With proper care, dental implants can last a lifetime. The crown on top typically lasts 15–20 years before needing replacement.

What is the cost of teeth whitening?

Professional in-clinic laser whitening at our studio starts at ₹8,000. We recommend calling or WhatsApping us for a personalized quote based on your current shade and goals.

Do you treat children?

Yes. We have a dedicated pediatric dentistry approach with techniques specifically designed to keep children calm and comfortable during visits.

Is there parking available at the clinic?

Yes, street parking is available outside the clinic. The clinic is also accessible by metro — we're a 10-minute walk from the GK metro station.

Do you offer EMI or payment options?

Yes, we offer 0% EMI on select procedures through major credit cards. Please ask our team during your consultation for details.
